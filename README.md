# Promiso - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 2025

Promiso ("we", "our", or "the app") is a promise tracking and accountability application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your data.

> **Key Point:** Your promises and personal data are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your promise content.

---

## Information We Collect

### Data Stored Locally
The following data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers:
- Promises you create (content, dates, categories)
- Your progress, streaks, and achievements
- App preferences and settings
- Notification preferences

### Data Collected by Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services that may collect certain data:
- **RevenueCat:** Manages subscriptions and purchases. Collects anonymous purchase data and subscription status.
- **Google AdMob:** Displays advertisements. May collect device identifiers and usage data for personalized ads.
- **Sentry:** Crash reporting and error tracking. Collects anonymous error logs to help us improve the app.

---

## How We Use Information
- To provide and maintain the app's functionality
- To process your premium subscription (if applicable)
- To display relevant advertisements (free version)
- To identify and fix bugs and crashes
- To improve user experience

---

## Advertisements
The free version of Promiso displays advertisements through Google AdMob. These ads may be personalized based on your interests and device information.

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings:
- **iOS:** Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising → Turn off Personalized Ads

Premium subscribers enjoy an ad-free experience.

---

## Data Security
- All local data is stored using secure device storage
- We do not store your personal promise data on any external servers
- Payment processing is handled securely by Apple through RevenueCat

---

## Children's Privacy
Promiso is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

---

## Your Rights
You have the right to:
- Delete all your local data by uninstalling the app
- Opt out of personalized advertising
- Request information about data collected by third-party services
- Contact us with any privacy concerns

---

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
